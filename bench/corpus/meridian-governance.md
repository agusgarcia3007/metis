# Meridian Edge Standard — Governance and Working Groups

The Meridian Edge Standard is stewarded by the Orrery Foundation, a non-profit chartered in the
city of Hallowmere. The Foundation does not write code; it ratifies specifications and accredits
auditors.

## Working groups

Each Meridian component is maintained by a dedicated working group:

- The **Lumen** component is maintained by the **Tessera** working group.
- The **Aster** component is maintained by the **Halyard** working group.
- The **Quill** component is maintained by the **Marlinspike** working group.
- The **Tideway** component is maintained by the **Bellwether** working group.

## Chairs

- The Tessera working group is chaired by **Dr. Ingrid Solvang**.
- The Halyard working group is chaired by **Professor Amara Okonkwo**.
- The Marlinspike working group is chaired by **Dr. Renzo Castellanos**.
- The Bellwether working group is chaired by **Dr. Yuki Tanabe**.

The four chairs together form the **Steering Quorum**, which must reach unanimous agreement before
any component's memory budget may be revised. The Steering Quorum meets twice a year in Hallowmere.

## Ratification

The current specification, version 4, was ratified in the year **2034**. The previous version,
version 3, was ratified in 2029. Each ratification requires a two-thirds majority of accredited
member organizations, of which there are currently 51.

## Accreditation

Auditors who certify Vermillion-tier builds must themselves be accredited by the Orrery Foundation
and must renew their accreditation every three years. An auditor who lets accreditation lapse may
not sign off on any conformance audit until it is restored.
